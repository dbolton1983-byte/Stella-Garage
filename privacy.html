Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Stella Garage ("we", "us", "our") handles information in connection with our website, content, and the internal tools we use to schedule and publish content to third-party social media platforms.

1. Information we handle

We operate a private, single-operator content tool. It is not a consumer product and does not collect personal information from visitors to this website. When we connect to a third-party platform (such as TikTok) to publish our own content, that platform issues us access credentials (tokens) tied to our own creator account. We use those credentials solely to upload and publish our own videos.

2. Platform data (e.g. TikTok)

When we authorize our tool with a platform, we may access basic account information for our own account and the ability to upload or publish videos to it. We use this access only to operate our own creator account. We do not access, collect, or store personal data belonging to other users of that platform.

3. How we use information

  • To schedule and publish our own content to our own accounts.
  • To review the performance of our own published content.
  • To maintain and secure our tools.

4. Storage & security

Access credentials are stored locally and privately, are never shared with third parties, and are not sold. We take reasonable measures to protect them and revoke them if they are no longer needed.

5. Sharing

We do not sell or share personal information. Content we choose to publish is, by its nature, made public on the relevant platform according to that platform's settings.

6. Third-party platforms

Platforms we publish to (such as TikTok and Fanvue) have their own privacy policies governing how they handle data. Please review their policies for details on their practices.

7. Data retention & revocation

You may revoke our tool's access to any connected platform at any time through that platform's settings, which invalidates our stored credentials for it.

8. Children

Our services are intended for adults and are not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required by your jurisdiction).

9. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates take effect when posted here.
